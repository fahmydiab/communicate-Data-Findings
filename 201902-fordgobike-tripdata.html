Dataset Overview¶

The dataset used for this exploratory analysis contains individual trip data for one month Feb. 2019 in CSV format. Visualizations below show exploration analysis and help discover usage pattern and rider characteristics.

Objective¶

My aim is to find the relation between the dataset features and the trip duration and the count of trips

# Creating weekday column and transforming the weekday to category type
weekday = ['Monday', 'Tuesday', 'Wednesday', 'Thursday', 'Friday', 'Saturday', 'Sunday']

df['start_dayofweek'] = pd.DatetimeIndex(df['start_time']).day_name()
df['start_dayofweek'] = df['start_dayofweek'].astype(weekdaycat)

Summary¶

  • The trip duration has a mean of 11 minutes and has a mode of duration (0-10) minutes
  • Most riders have the age from 30-40 years old
  • Higher percentage of female and other rides longer trips then males although number of male riders are very high then other and female.
  • The majority number of users are male .
  • Many trips concentrated around 7-10am and 17-19 pm on work days for both genders when used for work commute.
  • Other than the different usage goals, it may also have to do with the traffic situation around these rush hours in the area.
  • Female and other gender types rides durations are longer than that of Males.
